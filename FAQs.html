Want To Book Janelle?

Step 1

Email, call, or text us with pictures of the spaces you need help with. We will then get you a quote with how many hours + what product we think is needed to complete your project. We will also tell you how many team members will be needed.

Step 2

Book your date(s)! We will let you know available calender date(s). You let us know which work best for you. Send us your deposit and information and your appointment is booked. We'll be in touch as the organization booking date gets closer.

Step 3

A few weeks before your appointment we will connect, make sure we have an up to date visual on the space we are organizing, send you any product we need you to order direct, and connect on goals.

Step 4

We come do our thing! We will bring product (if needed) and implement systems that will be easy to maintain and ensure your space looks and stays aesthetically pleasing.
