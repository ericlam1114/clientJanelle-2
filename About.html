About Janelle

Janelle Cohen empowers people from all walks of life to regain control over their spaces and, by extension, their lives. A professional organizer, interior decorator, and entrepreneur, she has taken her personal passions and turned them into a thriving business that allows her to help others where - and when - they need it most. Her clients include household names such as Jordyn Woods (with whom her work was featured on MTV Cribs), Emma Chamberlain, Jay Shetty, and more, many of them everyday families looking to bring magic back into their day to day. Janelle has also been featured in numerous publications (People Magazine, Star Magazine, House Beautiful, Apartment Therapy) and brought unique yet accessible content to even more audiences through partnerships with beloved brands like Buzzfeed, Headspace, and The Container Store.

Janelle builds personal and intimate relationships with her clients, treating their space and their dreams for it as sacred; most are long-term, recurring relationships, and she's proud to be a part of each major milestone for those she works with as they progress through the years. Despite this, her goal is ultimately to not only help but also teach, creating sustainable solutions that others can maintain and replicate on their own. Janelle hopes to place the skills and tools she has developed over the years into the hands of countless others by codifying knowledge that is seldom taught but often sorely needed. In doing so, she continues to inspire individuals and families around the globe to take charge of their past, present, and future, starting with their own homes. Janelle is the author of “The Folding Book” coming out April 2022. You can preorder now on Amazon!
